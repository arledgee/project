Disneyland

"The Happiest Place On Earth"

The History of Disneyland

  • Disneyland was originally named "The Mickey Mouse Park.
  • Disneyland opened on July 17th, 1955.
  • It opened with 18 rides and attractions.
  • One year after Disneyland opened, 5 million people had visited it.
  • Disney California Adventure Park was originally a parking lot.

Fun Facts About Disneyland

  • There are dozens of stray cats around Disneyland.
  • The Matterhorn has a basketball court above the roller coaster ride.
  • The Indiana Jones Adventure was the most expensive ride to build.
  • The Pirates of the Caribbean ride is the most popular attraction at Disneyland.
  • Disneyland created the first daily operating monorail in the Western Hemisphere.
  • Disneyland is most known for the song/ride, "It's a Small World".
