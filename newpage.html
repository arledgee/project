About Utah






  1. History of Utah
    • The Utah Territory was established in 1850.
    • Utah became in 45th state admitted to the Union in 1896.
    • Brigham Young and the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints arrived in the Great Salt Lake Valley on July 24, 1847


  2. Fun Facts About Utah
    • Utah has five National Parks: Arches, Bryce Canyon, Canyonlands, Capitol Reef, and Zion.
    • Utah is the 2nd driest state in America.
    • The electric guitar was invented in Uath.
